# Design System Specification

## Overview
This design system is built on a foundation of professional clarity and balanced proportions. It utilizes a sophisticated "fidelity" color approach to ensure brand consistency across all UI components, balancing a deep, trustworthy primary teal with high-energy accent colors.

## Typography
The typographic system prioritizes modern legibility and a clean aesthetic.
- **Headlines**: Plus Jakarta Sans — A modern geometric sans-serif that lends a contemporary and professional voice to the interface.
- **Body & Labels**: Inter — Chosen for its exceptional readability on digital screens, providing a neutral and efficient container for information.

## Color Palette
The palette is designed for high contrast and functional clarity in a light mode environment.
- **Primary Color**: #005B71 (Deep Teal) — Used for primary actions, navigation elements, and brand identity.
- **Secondary Color**: #98FFD9 (Mint Green) — A vibrant support color for secondary UI elements and highlights.
- **Tertiary Color**: #F4A261 (Sand/Coral) — An accent color used for decorative elements, badges, or call-outs that require distinct visual separation.
- **Neutral**: Integrated through the light mode surface logic to maintain a clean, airy workspace.

## Layout & Geometry
The spatial system has been adjusted to provide a more standard, balanced feel.
- **Spacing**: 2 (Normal) — Moving from a compact layout to a standard spacing scale, providing a comfortable amount of whitespace that balances information density with editorial clarity.
- **Roundedness**: 1 (Subtle) — UI elements feature slight corner radiuses. This approach maintains a professional, precise, and organized appearance, avoiding the harshness of sharp corners while remaining more structured than pill-shaped designs.
